# pamgolet-sales-analysis
It Analyzes and visualize business performance for a company called Pamgolet across two core areas — sales and profits. The presentation spans five slides: a title slide establishing the source and timestamp, a summary slide consolidating key findings, a recommendations slide presenting actionable conclusions drawn from the analysis, a profits slide covering profitability performance including profit by product, segment, and country, and a sales slide examining revenue trends, top-performing products, and units sold across the years 2013 and 2014. Key highlights from the data include Paseo emerging as the best-selling and most profitable product with 338K units sold and $4.80M in profit, the United States leading all countries with $25.03M in total sales, the Government segment generating the highest profit at $11.4M, and the Enterprise segment recording a loss of $614.55K — with the group recommending that Pamgolet leverage Paseo's success, review Enterprise operations, capitalize on Government partnerships, and continue expanding in high-performing regions such as the U.S., Canada, and France.
